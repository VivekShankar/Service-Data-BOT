CPIM Livesite BOT

This bot will access data sources of different services (e.g.: CPIM Service data) and guide livesite engineers to effectively handle/mitigate issues. The BOT will be integrated with Skype and leverage Kusto APIs to provide insights into service health and diagnostics for CPIM.

Visit Bot Framework to register your bot. When you register it, remember to set your bot's endpoint to 

https://your_bots_hostname/api/messages
